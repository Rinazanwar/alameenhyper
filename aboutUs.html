About Al Ameen Hypermarket

since - Jan 26, 2011

AL AMEEN ” Means the most honest. Prophet Muhammed was Al Ameen, who never told a lie, even to his critics niether rivals, “AL AMEEN SUPER MARKET ” means a place where most honest trade takes place Amidst happiness, Let us go back a little behind of our past glorious days. Kuzhikkattil Traders, a high class provision store took its birth in 1970, in the heart of parappanangadi town. Since there, it started serving common people of parappanangadi with utmost sincerity and unbelievable authority in the market .Over a period of time, they got established in the minds of customers be cause they believed in serving the public , keeping the time schedule from 8am to 8pm every day except Sundays as being holiday, competitive pricing of goods, supply of high quality materials, never ending stocks of various things and so on, What not! Our rapport with publics went on smoothly as a result of which we were compelled, to come out with a whole sale store to make available every goods to the needy at a very considerable price. The wholesale store is AL AMEEN the most honest. Most genuine provisions goods selling house of kuzhikattil Traders. AL AMEEN Store knew the pulse of the market and made available the goods wanted by the common people. And one fine morning, Al ameen store inaugurated a super market by name AL AMEEN SUPER MARKET, to be precisely telling Jan26th, 2011,On republic day, the birth of AL AMEEN SUPER MARKET took place in Kadalundi Road at the starting point of the newly constructed Railway over bridge. A long cherished wish of the people of parappanangadi came true. As time passed, the kind hearted people of parappanangadi kept their faith in us and as did we. As a commitment to these people, we expanded our range and now AL AMEEN SUPER MARKET has become AL AMEEN HYPERMARKET.
